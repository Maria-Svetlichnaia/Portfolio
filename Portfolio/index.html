About me

Hello! As you may have guessed, I am Maria. Welcome to my portfolio website.

I am a Linguist and International Relations specialist, who decided to turn her life upside down and become a web developer.

I have rediscovered my web design interest and learnt that programming is fun and not as hard as I thought. After all, who knows, what is more difficult: the German language or JavaScript. If I mastered the former, I will definitely succeed in learning the latter.

I am a newbie, but I am also a passionate learner, careful listener and an ambitious person. So, if you want your ideas to be heard, implemented exactly the way you imagine and improved, then you have landed on the right page.

I am looking forward to meeting you and helping you make
your project come true.
